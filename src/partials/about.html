What is DVCHacks?

DVCHacks is a hackathon organized by Diablo Valley College students where anyone (yes, not just DVC students!) can make their dream project come true and compete for prizes! Join our 24 hours event filled with interesting workshops and fun activities.

  • 24 Hours Hackathon

  • Workshops from Sponsors

  • Networking Opportunities

  • Free Lunches & Dinner

  • Amazing Awards
